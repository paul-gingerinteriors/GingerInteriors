About Us

Ginger Interiors specializes in drylining construction, delivering high-quality solutions for both domestic and commercial projects. With a focus on precision and innovation, we transform spaces into functional and stylish environments.

Our expertise includes wall linings, ceiling installations, partitioning, acoustic treatments, and bespoke garden out houses. We work closely with trusted suppliers and partners across the UK to ensure every project meets the highest standards.

At Ginger Interiors, we are committed to excellence, reliability, and creating spaces that exceed client expectations.
