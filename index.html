About Us

Ginger Interiors is a leading drylining construction company with a commitment to quality and innovation. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering exceptional results for every project.

Paul Houghton, the driving force behind Ginger Interiors, brings years of expertise to the world of drylining construction. With a proven track record in both domestic and commercial projects, Paul is known for his meticulous attention to detail and innovative approach. From transforming homes into modern sanctuaries to delivering large-scale commercial solutions, his work speaks of quality and dedication. Paul also specializes in crafting bespoke garden out houses, creating functional and stylish spaces that perfectly blend with outdoor environments. His passion for excellence ensures every project is a masterpiece.

Shay Houghton, Paul's nephew, is an integral part of the Ginger Interiors team. With a fresh perspective and a passion for craftsmanship, Shay contributes to the company's innovative approach to drylining construction. His dedication and enthusiasm ensure that every project is completed to the highest standards, making him a valuable asset to the team.

Over the years, Paul has collaborated with major suppliers and companies across the UK, building a reputation for excellence and reliability. Now, with Shay joining the team, Ginger Interiors continues to strengthen these partnerships, delivering outstanding results for clients nationwide.
